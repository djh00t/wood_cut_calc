About Timber Species

Timber species information helps you organize your inventory and cutting plans by wood type.

Benefits of specifying species:

  • Match cuts to appropriate inventory items
  • Ensure consistent wood type throughout a project
  • Track specialized or premium woods separately

Common examples include: Pine, Oak, Maple, Cherry, Walnut, Birch, Ash, Cedar, etc.
